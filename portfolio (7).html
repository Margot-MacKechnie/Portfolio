About Me
Strategy. Sustainability. Innovation.

I'm a senior at Syracuse University's Whitman School of Management, studying Entrepreneurship & Emerging Enterprises with a minor in Information Technology, Design & Startups from the iSchool.

My work lives at the intersection of sustainability, technology, and business innovation — from green supply chain management to community-centered retail concepts to AI ethics policy.

I'm passionate about building ventures that create both economic and environmental value, and I'm actively seeking opportunities in sustainability consulting, supply chain management, and international markets — particularly in Nordic countries known for their leading-edge environmental policy.

My Mission

I believe the most powerful solutions sit at the intersection of innovation, technology, creativity, and sustainability — designed not just to minimize harm, but to actively create value for people and the planet alike. My work is driven by the conviction that business strategy and environmental responsibility are not trade-offs. They are the same goal.
