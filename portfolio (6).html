A proposed framework for ethical AI training data access, modeled on EU data privacy law (GDPR) and the music industry's licensing structure. The framework gives creators three pathways: opt-in licensing with compensation, explicit opt-out protection, and attribution-based passive consent — balancing innovation with intellectual property rights.
